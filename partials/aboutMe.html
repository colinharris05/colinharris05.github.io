Europe Trip

In the summer of 2013, my brother, his friend, and I took a month long backpacking trip throughout Europe. We traveled quite a lot of places in that month, staying in each city for roughly three days each. Here are some of the major places we went to:

  • Barcelona, Spain
  • Marseille, France
  • Nice, France
  • Rome, Italy
  • Florence, Italy
  • Venice, Italy
  • Munich, Germany
  • Prague, Czech Republic
  • Amsterdam
  • Salzburg, Austria
  • Vienna, Austria
  • Paris, France

We saw some pretty awesome places along the trip. Hopefully I can visit Europe again.
