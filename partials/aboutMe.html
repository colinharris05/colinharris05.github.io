How did I get into tech?

My older brother Devon Harris is a UW-Madison alum, working as a software engineer. He introduced me to the field and encouraged me to dip into some CS courses. I took the introductory programming classes as a freshman. After that I jumped into a Compilers course, where things started to make sense. After a few more courses and a summer internship, I decided to start doing some projects in my free time. Check out my Projects Page to read about some!

Europe Trip

In the summer of 2013, my brother, his friend and I took a month long backpacking trip throughout Europe. We traveled to quite a lot of places in that month, staying in each city for roughly three days each. Here are some of the major places we visted:

  • Barcelona, Spain
  • Marseille, France
  • Nice, France
  • Rome, Italy
  • Florence, Italy
  • Venice, Italy
  • Munich, Germany
  • Prague, Czech Republic
  • Amsterdam
  • Salzburg, Austria
  • Vienna, Austria
  • Paris, France

We saw some pretty amazing places along the trip. Hopefully I can visit Europe again.
